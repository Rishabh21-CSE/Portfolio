About Project

->Ecosphere is a application that helps users calculate their personal carbon footprint based on daily habits and lifestyle choices. It allows users to set sustainability goals, track their environmental impact, and visualize progress with dynamic graphs.

->Ecosphere enables users to calculate their carbon footprint from daily activities, set personal waste-reduction goals, visualise their impact over time with charts and data and encourage eco-friendly habits through goal tracking.

->Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, Ecosphere combines interactive forms with real-time data visualization to help users understand and reduce their carbon emissions. Future plans include integration with APIs for real-world environmental data.
